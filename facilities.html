Our Facilities

Eight venues, one address. Something for everyone.

Event Centre

Bookings Available
Photo Coming Soon

The Boardwalk Fun City Event Centre is the complex's flagship space — a versatile, fully equipped venue designed to accommodate occasions of every kind. From intimate private dinners to large-scale corporate functions, the centre adapts to suit your vision without losing its character.

The space comes furnished with professional sound and lighting infrastructure, flexible seating arrangements, and an in-house coordination team ready to manage the details that tend to escape notice — until they go wrong. Our preference is that they never do.

Whether you are launching a product, celebrating a milestone, hosting a conference, or organizing a live performance, the Event Centre gives you the room and the support to make it happen well.

Arcade

Walk-In Welcome
Photo Coming Soon

The arcade at Boardwalk Fun City spans classic and contemporary titles across dozens of machines — a deliberate mix that caters to people who grew up with joysticks and those who arrived later. Competition, nostalgia, and a certain kind of unguarded fun are equally at home here.

The space is well-maintained, well-lit, and designed for extended play. It is equally suited to a solo visit and a group outing. No booking is required — simply walk in and find your machine.

Bowling Alley

Lane Bookings Available
Photo Coming Soon

Our professional-grade bowling lanes are built for serious play and relaxed evenings alike. Whether you are looking for a casual lane with friends or want to organize a proper tournament with standings and prizes, the alley can accommodate both.

Lanes can be booked privately for group outings, or joined during open-play sessions — a good option for solo visitors or pairs who want the rhythm of a shared space without the formality of a reservation. Shoe hire is available at the counter.

Bar

Open Daily
Photo Coming Soon

The Boardwalk Fun City bar operates on the belief that a good drink, served properly, is its own form of hospitality. Our menu spans crafted cocktails, curated spirits, a considered wine list, and cold lager — because not every evening calls for the same glass.

The atmosphere is refined without being stiff. You can come straight from the bowling alley still wearing your rented shoes and feel entirely at ease. The bar is as good a reason to arrive as any facility in the complex, and a worthy reason to stay a little longer than planned.

Pool

Open During Complex Hours
Photo Coming Soon

The swimming pool is well-maintained and available for recreational use and lap swimming throughout regular opening hours. Poolside seating and service are available — a good arrangement if you prefer to be near the water without necessarily being in it.

The pool can also be reserved for private events, making it an appealing option for birthday celebrations or group outings that want a setting with a little more personality than a standard event room. Contact us for private booking rates and availability.

Barbershop

Walk-Ins Welcome
Photo Coming Soon

The Boardwalk Fun City barbershop is staffed by skilled hands and operates with the kind of quiet professionalism that puts you at ease the moment you sit down. Services cover precision cuts, fades, beard grooming, and general styling for men and boys.

No appointment is necessary — walk in between other activities at the complex and leave looking considerably better for it. For those who prefer to plan ahead, we recommend arriving early on weekends when demand tends to be higher.

Lounge

Open Daily
Photo Coming Soon

The lounge is the social heart of Boardwalk Fun City — the place where an afternoon can turn into an evening without anyone quite noticing. Comfortable seating, ambient music, and attentive service make it an easy place to settle in, whether you are meeting someone, waiting for a lane, or simply unwinding with no particular agenda.

It is also, quietly, one of the best spots in the complex to observe the full life of the building — the kind of place that reminds you why mixed-use spaces exist. Everyone ends up here eventually.

5-a-Side Football Pitch

Bookings by the Hour
Photo Coming Soon

A floodlit, all-weather five-a-side pitch available for hourly bookings, casual pickup games, and organized small-sided tournaments. The surface is well-maintained and the lines are clearly marked — conditions that serious players tend to notice and appreciate.

Pitch hire is available in hourly slots. Private tournament packages — including coordination, referee, and use of adjacent lounge space — can be arranged through our events team. Contact us to check availability and reserve your slot.
