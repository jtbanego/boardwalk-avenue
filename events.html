The Event Centre

Built for Every Occasion

◆

The Boardwalk Fun City Event Centre is Abuja's most versatile private hire venue — a space that takes your brief seriously and delivers on it.

We understand that an event is rarely just about the space. It is the lighting that creates the right mood, the sound system that doesn't let you down mid-speech, the coordination that keeps the evening moving without making you feel managed. These are the details our team exists to handle, so that you can be present for the parts that matter.
