About City of David

Everything You Need,
In One Place

◆

City of David is more than a complex — it is an address that Abuja will come to know by heart. Whether you arrive for a night out, a sporting match, a private event, or simply to sit and breathe, you will find something here that is genuinely yours.

Eight distinct venues share one building, which means your evening can move from the bowling alley to the bar to the lounge without ever stepping outside. Boardwalk Avenue is the address. City of David is the destination.

Our Facilities

Eight World-Class Experiences

◆

Event Centre

A fully equipped venue for corporate gatherings, private celebrations, product launches, and live performances.

Arcade

Classic and contemporary titles across dozens of machines — a space for competition, nostalgia, and genuine fun.

Bowling Alley

Professional-grade lanes suited to casual outings and organized tournaments. Book a lane or join open play.

Bar

Crafted cocktails, curated spirits, and a thoughtful selection of wines and beers in a refined, relaxed setting.

Pool

A well-maintained swimming facility open for recreational use, lap sessions, and private poolside events.

Barbershop

Precision cuts and grooming services in a calm, comfortable setting. Walk in and leave looking your best.

Lounge

The social heart of the complex. Comfortable seating, ambient music, and attentive service throughout the day.

5-a-Side Football Pitch

A floodlit, all-weather pitch for five-a-side matches. Book by the hour or organize a private tournament.
